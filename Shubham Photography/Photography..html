A LITTLE BIT ABOUT ME

Nature photography is a genre of photography focused on elements of the outdoors. Specific subjects may include sky, water, and land, or the flora and fauna that inhabit these spaces—or both. I became a wedding photographer because I fell in love with the authenticity of the day, the planning process, the butterflies a bride get's before she walks down the aisle & all the unexpected happy moments that come along with a wedding day.
